# Pixie - Brand & Design Guidelines (V3: Soft Pastel Squircles)

This document defines the highly custom, uniquely tailored "Soft Pastel Squircles" aesthetic for Pixie. We are moving away from sharp, cold, high-contrast dark modes, and fully embracing an ultra-soft, organic, and welcoming interface.

## 1.0 Design Philosophy
- **Organic Curves (Squircles):** Interfaces should feel like perfectly smooth, organic pebbles. We are using massive border radiuses.
- **Warm & Fresh Palettes:** Backgrounds are not grey or solid white. They are subtly tinted with warmth (like soft sage or vanilla) so they don't strain the eyes.
- **Gentle Contrast:** Deep charcoals instead of pure black. Soft pastels for energy instead of harsh neons. 

## 2.0 Color Palette

### Core Environment
- **Premium Background**: Soft Sage/Mint Tint (`#f4f7f4`) - replacing the cold grey.
- **Card Background (Light)**: Pure White (`#ffffff`) or Frosted Glass.
- **Card Background (Dark)**: Deep Charcoal Solid (`#222222`).

### The Soft Pastel Accents
- **Primary Energy**: Mint Green (`#a7f3d0`). Used for massive CTA areas or prominent statistics.
- **Secondary Magic**: Gentle Lilac (`#c4b5fd`). Used for contrasting blocks against the mint.
- **Text Primary**: Soft Black (`#111827`).
- **Text Muted**: Ash (`#6b7280`).

## 3.0 Typography
- **Headers & Display**: `Plus Jakarta Sans`. Excellent for highly rounded, friendly text.
- **Body & UI**: `Inter`, cleanly spaced.

## 4.0 Component Styling (The "Squircle" Rule)
- **Border Radius**: Absurdly smooth. Default massive cards should be **`48px`** (`3rem`). Smaller interior elements should be **`24px`**.
- **Borders**: No harsh outlines. We rely on whitespace and extremely soft diffused shadows.
- **Shadows**: Floating, diffused shadows: `box-shadow: 0 30px 60px -15px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.05)`.
- **Containers**: Allow elements a massive amount of breathing room. Internal padding inside cards should be enormous (e.g., `2.5rem` to `3rem`).
